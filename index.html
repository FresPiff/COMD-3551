About the site

Welcome to Sketchview, a website dedicated to providing reviews of all kinds of sketchbooks, written by artists, for artists! Wether you're just starting out or are looking to try something new, our reviews will provide you with in-depth information that might just make your next choice of sketchbook the best one for you!
